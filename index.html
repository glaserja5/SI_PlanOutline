Sales Insight Dashboard Development Roadmap

Phase 1: Research and Product Analysis 2-3 Days
Deep dive into the sales pipeline, define Sales Insight purpose, understand user needs, and analyze existing solutions.
Key Notes
  • Outline sales pipeline from lead to close in formal document
  • Talk to David/Dad about sales processes and pain points for clients who use either sales insight or other software
  • Analyze and take notes on large competitors (Salesforce, HubSpot) to identify design practices
  • Write up a full document on current workflow inefficiencies and bugs, new features, dashboards,
  • Overview the current database structure and what data is available for analyses
Deliverables
  • Pipeline Guide: Start to finish overview of the concrete sales pipeline
  • Sales Insight MVP Planning Document
    • Identify problem in sales pipeline and the SI solution
    • Overview of current SI issues, inefficiencies, areas for improvement
    • Overview of alternative CRM features and architecture
    • List of potential modules in the sales insight system (KPI's, Mapping, Sales tracking etc.) and then outline their functionalities, designs, and data requirements
    • Gather comprehensive list of stakeholder feedback and needs from whatever stakeholders involved so I know the needs. David relays this info to me.
Additional Details
Communication:
Set up weekly meeting with David to gather and verify information for MVP Planning Document
